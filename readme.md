# Observer

Observer is a platform built to help NGOs and activist groups support forcibly displaced
individuals and animals. It provides a secure, organized way to manage cases, track
humanitarian support, and store sensitive personal information — replacing the chaos of
unprotected spreadsheets with a proper system.

## Why it exists

When people flee conflict or disaster, the organizations helping them often rely on
spreadsheets to manage everything — personal data, documents, support history. These grow
unwieldy fast, offer no protection for sensitive information, and make it nearly impossible
to get a clear picture of any individual case.

Observer gives those organizations a dedicated tool: one place to register displaced people
and animals, manage their cases, track the support they've received, and do it all with
the security and auditability that handling vulnerable people's data demands.

## Who it's for

NGOs, volunteer groups, and activist organizations working with displaced people and animals
who need a structured, secure alternative to spreadsheets.
